This is the greatest Dice Landing Page!

  • Features

    • A traditional die is a cube with each of its six faces marked with a different number of dots (pips) from one to six. When thrown or rolled, the die comes to rest showing a random integer from one to six on its upper surface, with each value being equally likely.

  • How It Works

    • The player shooting the dice is the first to bet and the other players must at least match his bet before the game can continue. If the bet cannot be matched, the shooter can either lower the bet to accommodate the other players, or handicap the odds.

  • Pricing

    • It's pretty standard to charge $0.50 to $1.00 for individual dice. If you need a lot of dice you can get much better deals buying in bulk though.
